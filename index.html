How to Use Write CX

Three simple ways to enhance your writing experience

Chrome Extension - Translate & Optimize Anywhere

1. Install Chrome Extension

Add Write CX to your Chrome browser from the Chrome Web Store.

2. Select Text on Any Website

Highlight any text you want to optimize or translate.

3. Click the Write CX Icon

Access Write CX directly from your browser toolbar.

4. Choose Your Action

Select whether to translate, optimize, or perform other actions.

5. Get Instant Results

See your optimized text immediately and copy it with one click.

Quick Translation & Refinement

1. Accessing Settings Panel

Open the Write CX extension and click on Settings.

2. Setting Style Preferences

Choose your preferred writing tone and style.

3. Creating Custom Instructions

Write specific instructions for how you want your text processed.

4. Saving Your Preferences

Save your settings to apply them to all future optimizations.

5. Experiencing Personalized Results

See how your text is optimized according to your preferences.
