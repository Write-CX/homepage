Learn more about Write CX

Write CX provides multilingual writing support through a browser extension and web application. You can customize your AI assistant's behavior, set personalized instructions, and use them on any webpage. All settings are saved, allowing you to maintain a consistent writing experience anywhere, anytime.
The free version provides a fixed number of daily generations, sufficient for regular use. You can access three basic features: Reply, Improve, and Translate. If you need more usage or custom features, you can upgrade to the paid version anytime.
We take user privacy seriously. Write CX doesn't store your writing content; all interactions are real-time. Your personalized settings are securely encrypted and accessible only to you. For detailed information, please check our privacy policy.
Write CX supports writing and translation in all major languages. You can get high-quality AI assistance regardless of your language. The system automatically detects your language and provides appropriate support.
You can cancel your subscription anytime in account settings. After cancellation, your account will automatically convert to the free version at the end of the current billing cycle with no additional charges. All saved personalized settings will be retained.
